Our Certified Refurbishment Process

How we take retired corporate gear and breathe new life into it.

1

De-branding & Clean

Outer casing is cleaned, sanitized, and cosmetic checks are run. Corporate labels are safely removed.

2

Hardware Audit

Engineers benchmark CPU, stress-test motherboard components, check battery health, and verify screen pixels.

3

Upgrades Installed

Upgraded with brand new NVMe SSD storage and maximum-spec RAM configurations. OS is freshly re-installed.

4

Premium Packaging

Placed in our signature velvet sleeve inside the ReNova box, containing upgraded cords and bespoke welcome kits.
